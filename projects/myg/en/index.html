Who are we?

We are a group of six friends which play League of Legends as a hobby. We've been playing LOL for over two years and since Clash came out, we've decided to create a team: Mystical Gaming. For about a year now, we've been playing every Clash we can, and we've had a lot of fun together. So far we have won two Clashes and were often in the top three.
