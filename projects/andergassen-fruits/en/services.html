    Services and guaranteed quality

    Quality control

    Maintaining your quality standards is a matter of course for us. We guarantee our customers the highest level of service, quality, freshness and variety. There are guidelines agreed with the certified producers regarding appearance, sorting, ripeness, fruit flesh firmness and the control of pesticide residues. These guidelines are constantly checked on site. In addition, attention is also paid to general food safety, environmental protection and workers.

    Delivery on schedule

    Thanks to good cooperation with solid transport companies, we have an efficient logistics system, which enables us to deliver complete loads through to pallets of goods within the agreed deadlines. Speed, flexibility and reliability have absolute priority.

    Environmentally conscious cultivation

    The cultivation of the products is subject to strict production conditions. This applies to the selection of cultivation areas and plant material as well as to the use of fertilisers and plant protection material. In addition, the use of a balanced range of natural measures brings about an ecological balance. Growers must produce on the farm according to strict hygiene rules. The different areas of application, such as personal hygiene, buildings, harvesting and transport, are strictly controlled.

    Marketing and sales promotion

    Constant observation of the market, continuous monitoring of the goods and innovative sustainable packaging solutions - this is how we can continue to meet the high demands of our customers in the future.
