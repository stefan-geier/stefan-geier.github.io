    Our fruit agency has been founded in 1959 by Adalbert Andergassen and has evolved, over the years, into an internationally operating company. During these many years of activity we have always tried to act according current market conditions as well as to meet all of our customer's needs and requests with care and far-sightedness.

    70 years of experience

    Our activity

    Our activity covers the entire fruit and vegetable trade from Italy. Over 70 years of experience in the production, logistics and distribution of fruit and vegetables throughout Europe, are today the capital of our company.

    Our producers

    We maintain long-standing business relationships with proven producers whose innovative know-how and cultivation methods enable us to procure fresh, healthy and excellent-quality fruit and vegetables for you at prices in line with the market. When growing fruit and vegetables, producers must pay special attention to the selection of land, the plant material used and the careful use of plant protection products in harmony with the ecological balance. Producers are certified through comprehensive checks on quality and pesticide residues.

    Your success

    Our motivated team follows and informs you daily about the market situation and availability of all products. Every enquiry and every order you entrust to us is followed up with care and responsibility. The best possible offer for the best quality is the key to your success.
